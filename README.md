## Signals

- **LLMs as operators.** My work treats language models as decision-making systems, not chat interfaces: telemetry in, reasoning over time, action out.
- **Adversarial autonomy.** KSPDG is a real-time, multi-agent control environment with pursuit/evasion, uncertainty, and live opponent behavior; repeated podium performance matters more than a one-off benchmark.
- **Real satellite path.** The spacecraft-operator stack evolved from KSPDG simulation into supervised satellite-operations workflows for AVS missions including LUR-1 and UND-1/UND-2.
- **National defense thesis.** [Best Bachelor Thesis for National Defense](https://www.upm.es/UPM?id=CON16695&prefmt=articulo&fmt=detail), awarded by the UPM-CESEDEN Chair for **Language Models are Spacecraft Operators**.
- **MIT PhD-track signal.** Admitted to MIT AeroAstro SM on the PhD track; researching autonomy and robotics at the MIT Astrodynamics, Space Robotics, and Controls Lab.

## Vision

I want to understand and build AI architectures as cognitive systems: agents that perceive state, hold context, reason over time, decide under uncertainty, and improve through interaction.

The important frontier is deployment. Spacecraft, factories, robots, and operator workflows are where reasoning quality becomes measurable.
